Utilization of Phosphogypsum in ceramic industries

• Phosphogypsum(PG) is side product of phosphoric acid plant. By treating phosphate rock (import from Jordan) with sulfuric acid we get gypsum as product.
• But in GACL phosphate rock treating with Hydrochloric acid for the production of phosphoric acid and we get chlorinated phosphogypsum as side product.
• So we treated this chlorinated phosphogypsum with sulfuric acid at appropriate condition to produce product as useful material phosphogypsum(PG). We perform numbers of experiment to improve the quality of phosphogypsum(PG).
• It is use to make fly ash bricks adding other nutrients. And analyze the product by various methods like composition test, strength test, water absorption test etc.

The major goal of this project is to build option to Utilization of waste material from GACL company, likes other ceramics and that’s related companies. There are many thing learn during this project, like analytical skill, team work and project management and planning.
